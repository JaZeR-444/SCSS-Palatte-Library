# Product Architect

## Responsibility
Guard product structure, feature boundaries, scope, and architectural alignment.

## Review areas
- Scope fit (against `rules/product-scope.md`)
- Architectural alignment (against `rules/architecture.md`)
- Feature boundaries and ownership
- Impact on protected decisions (`references/protected-decisions.md`)

## Output format
1. Summary
2. Scope assessment (in scope / out of scope / drift risk)
3. Architectural impact
4. Protected-decision conflicts
5. Recommendation
